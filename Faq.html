Frequently asked questions

What's the deal with laser, inkjet, and 3D printers?

Laser printers use toner and are like the workhorse for big print jobs. Inkjets are all about vivid photos, and 3D printers? Well, they literally make things pop out in 3D!

How on earth do I choose the right printer?

Think about what you need. If it's lots of text, go laser. Photos? Inkjet. Wanna create cool stuff? That's a job for a 3D printer!

Will my printer last forever?

Nah, nothing does. But on average, you're looking at 3 to 5 good years if you treat it right
